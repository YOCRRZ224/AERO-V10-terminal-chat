About AERO-V10

AERO-V10 is a terminal-style, material-inspired chat and media platform. It allows multiple users to chat in real-time, stream music or radio, and use custom plugins for interactive experiences. Fully compatible with Android (Termux/Pydroid) and Linux systems, it combines aesthetics with powerful functionality.

Real-time Chat

Communicate with multiple users seamlessly in a futuristic terminal interface.

Music Streaming

Stream music & radio with dynamic queue, skip, and volume controls.

Custom Plugins

Add commands & interactive tools to extend the system functionality.

Multi-platform

Works on Android, Linux, and other terminals with full responsiveness.

Voice & Media Ready

Support for voice chat and rich media integration in future updates.
